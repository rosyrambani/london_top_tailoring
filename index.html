London Top Tailoring specializes in custom made clothing, dry cleaning and alteration services. Come in for reliable service by professionals with several years of experience. London, Ontario's authentic clothing alteration center.

Custom made clothing

Hamid takes numerous measurements to find the best fit profile for your clothing.You can also choose your own cloth type and only pay for stitching. Feel confident with custom tailored suits and dresses. Your attire makes you stand out of the crowd so why not get the best for yourself!

Dry Cleaning

Good looking clothes make you look good. We make sure that your clothes have a crisp fresh feeling. We offer environment friendly Dry-Cleaning Service. Corporates and government organisations can rely on quality and affordable service.

Alteration

  • Resizing suits, pants, coats, etc. for changes in body shape
  • Replacement or resewing of buttons on jackets, suits, shirts, etc.
  • Jean alterations and reattachment of original hem
  • Zipper replacement
  • Dress alterations

Our happy customers

London Top Tailoring is located in London,Ontario and offers custom made clothing, dry cleaning and alterations. Ask us any question you have. We would love to hear from you.
